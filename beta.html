TapChat BETA

Finally: Mobile IRC done right.

Thanks for interest in TapChat!

Stay connected with your favorite channels in the cloud instead of draining your phone's battery. Scroll back any time to catch up on what you've missed, and receive push notifications when someone mentions or messages you.

Getting Started

  1. Grab the Android app.
  2. Install the Irssi plugin: 
    $ curl -L https://goo.gl/ddgWk | bash
  3. ...or sign up for IRCCloud.

Known Issues

  • Android app
    • No app icon or design - please excuse the mess.
    • TapChat may not disconnect if the screen is turned off while the app is visible. Return home before sleeping your device.
