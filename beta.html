Thanks for your interest in TapChat!

Stay connected with your favorite channels in the cloud instead of draining your phone's battery. Scroll back any time to catch up on what you've missed, and receive push notifications when someone mentions or messages you.

Getting Started

  1. Grab the Android app.
  2. Set up the Irssi plugin or sign up for IRCCloud.
